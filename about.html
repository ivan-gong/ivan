Ivan Gong's Self Introduction

My name is Ivan Gong, a Cornell University student from China. Although I'm only in my sophomore year, I've been shooting all kinds of photos since 3rd grade. My favourite is landscape photography, but I also do portrait, street, and even wildlife photography sometimes. I don't want to be concentrated on only one type of topics; I always want to try something new. Photography is not only an art of using light, but also an art of using your mind. I'm not a professional, but I try my best to pursuit aesthetic whenever I press the shutter.
